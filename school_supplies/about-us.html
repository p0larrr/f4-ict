Microsoft Teams

Microsoft Teams is the ultimate messaging app for organization—a workspace for real-time collaboration and communication, meetings, file and app sharing, and even the occasional emoji.All in one place, all in the open, all accessible to everyone.

Using Teams, staff can share files, organize meetings from their calendar, and sync with other Office apps like OneNote, OneDrive, and Skype for Business. This improves collaboration and communication while simultaneously aiding the adoption of Office 365 Teams combines most every tool we use, all on one platform. With Teams, every file you send is securely stored in it's original Chat, Team or Channel via SharePoint or OneNote's smooth integrations.
